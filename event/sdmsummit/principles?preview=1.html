Guiding Principles

In order for the discussion at the Summit to be a success, representatives and their organizations will need to align with the following objective and principles:

Help patients make the best treatment decision possible

…regardless of whether that treatment is a drug, device, surgery, lifestyle change, etc

…regardless of who manufactures or provides that treatment
