Step 2: Lodging & Meals

Mohonk Mountain House Resort (map)

  • A Victorian castle resort, designated as "One of the Top Destinations in the World” by National Geographic Traveler Magazine (see other awards) 
  • Located in the Hudson Valley, near the Catskills, 90 miles north of NYC
  • Reservation includes all meals, prepared by award-winning chefs
  • $369 daily rate (the Summit will be 2 "days" at this rate)
  • Special rate above available 1 night before and after the Summit (call Mohonk directly to book extra nights)

By clicking the RESERVE link above, you acknowledge the policy below, which applies to the Summit and supercedes Mohonk's standard policy.
 
Check in/out Times
Check-in time: 4:00 p.m.
Check-out time: 1:00 p.m.
 
Conference Rate
Single Occupancy $369.00 per night
Double Occupancy $538.00 for two people, per night
Room rates will be honored for 3 days before the conference and 1 day after the conference based on availability.
 
Nightly Rate Information:
These rates are Full American plan rates, which include three consecutive meals daily, beginning with dinner on the day of arrival through lunch on the day of departure. Credit is not extended for missed meals.
 
Taxes & Administrative Fee
Tax excluded State Tax 8%, Occupancy Tax 1.7%, Administrative Fee 12% will be added to your bill. Additional daily charges will apply for each additional adult or child in the room.
 
Special Concessions
High Speed Internet Access (Wireless) provided throughout the conference
Valet parking is complementary for conference attendees
 
Guarantee Policy
Your reservation is guaranteed to your deposit for late arrival.
 
Cancellation Policy
In order to enjoy this special conference rate, cancellations less than 30 days from arrival date shall be charged the full rates for the conference attendance days (Sept 27 & 28).
 
Click "RESERVE" above to be directed to the Mohonk Mountain House website to reserve you room.
 
Alternatively, you may call the Mohonk Mountain House directly at 1.800.772.6646 and let them know you are with Society of Decision Professionals.
