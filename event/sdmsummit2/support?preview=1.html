Supporting the Summit

Enabling better healthcare decision making requires many people and types of organizations working together.  We are excited to partner with organizations that have an affinity for the mission and principles of the SDM Summit.  There are 3 categories of support, allowing organizations to select the best fit for them. 

Category Organizational Best Fit  Think of this as your org's chance to..
Grants Industry and for-profits "Put your money where your mouth is" and help ensure the Summit happens each year
Sponsorships Industry and for-profits
 "Roll up your sleeves" and get to work with us in shaping the Summit
Endorsements Non-profits and government Put your "seal of support" on the Summit, demonstrating your commitment

The Advisory Committee meets regularly to help shape the Summit agenda and topics.  Some Advisory Council members will act as stewards for a given topic/session.  They will have primary responsibility to develop a detailed description and direction for their topic.  More information about the Advisory Committee can be found in the ORGANIZATION section on the ABOUT page.

If your organization might be interested in supporting the Summit, please contact us at support@sdmsummit.org.
