Lodging Details & Transportation

The conference will be held at the Banff Park Lodge 
(222 Lynx Street P.O. Box 2200, Banff, Alberta, Canada T1L 1K5)

 

Room Rate

The conference room rate for the Superior Rooms is CAD $125+taxes per night for check-in on April 5 for those attending the SDP workshops and April 6 for those attending DAAG with check out on April 9. 

To reserve your room, click Here or copy and paste the URL in your browser https://www.banffparklodge.com/

*  Individuals wanting to stay any additional nights prior or post conference, please call the Banff Park Lodge directly toll-free number at 1-800-661-9266 (24h).

Check-out Time: 11:00 a.m.

How to get to the Banff Park Lodge

Banff is located at 222 Lynx Street P.O. Box 2200 Banff, Alberta, Canada T1L 1K5 in Alberta's Rockies and east of Lake Louise, approximately 90 minutes drive from Calgary International Airport (YYC).

 

Banff Airporter

DAAG attendees receive a 15% discount on the Banff Airporter for shuttle service between the Calgary Airport and Banff.

One way: CAD $54.00/person inclusive of taxes
Two way: CAD $108.00/person inclusive of taxes

To receive the discount, simply click on www.banffairporter.com/book .  Proceed through the reservation and type - DAAG - in the Apply Promotional Code section on the final payment page.
