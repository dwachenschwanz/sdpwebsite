Pre-Conference Workshops

There are 3 half-day workshops on Wednesday, March 15, 2017.

Workshop 1 (morning): Value-Focused Decision-Making , presented by Ralph Keeney of Duke University

Workshop 2 (afternoon): Value of Information, presented by Ellen Coopersmith and Luis Mendoza of Decision Frameworks.

Workshop 3 (afternoon): Building Organizational Decision Quality, presented by Bruce Judd and Carl Spetzler from Strategic Decision Group

Workshop fees:

  • One half-day workshop:   USD 300 per person
  • Two half-day workshops:  USD 450 per person

A light lunch is included in the workshop fee.
