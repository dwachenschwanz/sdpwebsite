VIRTUAL SDP ANNUAL CONFERENCE & WORKSHOPS

(Formerly Known As DAAG)

APRIL 27-29, 2022
VIRTUAL 

 

 

The 28th SDP Annual Conference & Workshops (Formerly DAAG Annual Conference & Workshops) will now be held as an all virtual format including the workshops.  To accommodate the abundant agenda, the conference has shifted from a 2-day event to a 3-day happening April 27-29, 2022 during the hours of 8:00am - 2:30pm (PT) with pre/post conference virtual workshops.

We know that ideally getting together in person for the conference is a much more empowering experience (and we were all looking forward to it) - however, given all the uncertainty around Corona and its variants, the decision came down to our responsibility of promoting the health & wellness for each of us.  Eliminating the face-to-face portion of the conference wasn't an easy decision.  The peace of mind that comes with allowing our speakers and attendees to attend safely from home is worth the shift.

The Virtual SDP Annual Conference is your chance to:

  • Hear from some of the most accomplished and thought provoking speakers in the decision-making profession.
  • Gain insight into emerging strategies, the latest innovative technologies, and best practices to move your skills to the next level.
  • Network with thought leaders, collaborate with your peers and build your professional network.
