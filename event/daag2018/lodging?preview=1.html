How to get there

The Coast Coal Harbour Hotel is located at 1180 West Hastings Street , Vancouver, Canada, approximately 10 miles from Vancouver International Airport (YVR).

Taxi: Taxis and wheelchair-accessible vehicles are available at taxi stands located on Level 2 of the Domestic and International Arrivals Area at YVR. Taxi rides cost approximately CAD $35.00 to the hotel.

Public Transportation: The Canada Line is Vancouver’s rapid transit rail connecting YVR to downtown Vancouver in under 30 minutes. Cost is approximately CAD $9.00 one-way. Canada Line’s YVR Airport station is centrally located between the International and Domestic Terminals. Take the train from the airport to Waterfront Station, transfer to Burrard Station and the hotel is about 2 blocks from there. Otherwise, you could walk from Waterfront Station, about a ten minute walk. Check the Canada Line website for the latest schedules, news and events.
