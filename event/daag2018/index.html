Introduction and Background

The SDP 2018 DAAG Conference will be held on April 12-13 and will be preceded by two days of  Pre-conference SDP Workshops on April 10-11 at the Coast Coal Harbour Hotel, Vancouver, Canada.

The Decision Analysis Affinity Group (DAAG) is an informal group of decision professionals who gathered annually since 1995 to share ideas on the application and practice of decision analysis. Their only organization structure was the committee that planned the following year's conference, Therefore the term "DAAG" is mostly used to describe both the event and the people that attend it. When SDP formed, a decision was made to neither duplicate nor compete with this practitioner conference. "DAAG” is now the annual conference of SDP, organized and run by SDP. It brings together decision professionals and decision makers from multiple industries to share ideas, successes, and failures. In addition, each year SDP organizes a day of workshops on the day prior to, and in the same location as the conference.

The organizing committee for the SDP 2018 DAAG Conference is chaired by Pat Leach and Len Falsone.

Conference Sessions

Session topics include:

  • From Data to Decisions – the Use of Big Data
  • Decision-Driven Risk Management
  • Leveraging Failure
  • Culture and Decision Quality
  • Involving Communities of Interest in the Decision Process
  • Emotions and DA: Is there a place for emotions and meaning in decision analysis?
  • Embedding Decision Quality 

There will also be a special session for those who are new to DAAG and the SDP.  Several sessions will feature embedded case studies. Details on the conference program can be found here.

Keynotes: This year’s conference includes keynotes by Hal Kvisle, former CEO of Trans Canada and former CEO of Talisman, and Eleanor Bergin, Economics and Decision Analysis Advisor for Hess Corporation. Details on the keynotes can be found here

Social Activities:  The logistics committee has done a fabulous job of preparing the conference venue, facilities, amenities, and evening activities. A conference reception on Wednesday night is included in the conference fee, as are breakfast and lunch during the conference.
