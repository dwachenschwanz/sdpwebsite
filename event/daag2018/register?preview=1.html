Pre-Conference Workshops

There are 4 half-day workshops on April 10 & 11, 2018.

Workshop 1 (10 April morning): The Skills and Science of Facilitative Questioning, presented by Katherine Rosback of KRE Inc. 

Workshop 2 (10 April afternoon): Leading Decision Conversations: Improving Projects through Better Decision Clarity, presented by Frank Koch of Koch Decision Consulting

Workshop 3 (11 April morning): Ethics in Decision Making,Presented by Dr. Ron Howard of Stanford University.

Workshop 3 (11 April afternoon): Social Change Without Coercion, presented by Dr. Ron Howard of Stanford University.
