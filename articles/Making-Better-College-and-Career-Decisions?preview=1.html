Making Better College and Career Decisions

Frank Koch, Koch Decisions and Amy Stranieri, Counseling Dept. Chair, Thurston High School
Oct 19, 2016 (SDP Webinar Invited Talk)

For most students, the college decision is the first major strategic decision of their lives. Thurston High School has offered a class that incorporates basic Decision Quality with improving the students’ ability to successfully apply to and be accepted at the college of their choice. This presentation describes the specific impact the class has had on several students’ decisions and the techniques and tools that have proven most useful in improving their decisions.
