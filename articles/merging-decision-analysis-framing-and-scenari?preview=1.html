Merging Decision Analysis Framing and Scenario Planning for More Robust Strategy Development 

Presented by Ellen Coopersmith – SDP Fellow 
(SDP Webinar - Invited Talk)

This presentation introduces a merged Decision Analysis Framing – Scenario Planning workflow, using a business unit strategy example,set in a highly restrictive business environment. Emphasis is placed on understanding how to execute each step of the workflow and providing tips for successful execution.
