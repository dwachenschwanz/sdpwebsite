New to DQ (Decision Quality)?

Carl Spetzler, Strategic Decisions Group
February 28, 2024 (SDP Invited Talk)

Abstract: The Society of Decision Professionals is committed to making DQ accessible to all decision makers and decision support professionals. At our upcoming conference we will have both an introductory DA workshop and a one-day track for anyone new to DQ and the profession. In this webinar, you will get a quick preview to help you decide to join and/or invite others to join us in Arlington, Virginia from April 15 - 19, 2024. The webinar will be moderated by David Matheson, president of the SDP and features Carl Spetzler, chairman of Strategic Decisions Group.
