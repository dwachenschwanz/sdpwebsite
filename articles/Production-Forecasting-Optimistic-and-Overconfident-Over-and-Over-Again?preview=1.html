Production Forecasting: Optimistic and Overconfident – Over and Over Again

San Francisco Chapter Meeting: Speaker Reidar Bratvold, University of Stavanger
July 10, 2020

This talk discusses collaborative work done at University of Stavanger and University of Texas to compare production forecasting at the time of investment decisions versus actual production, and to asses whether the forecastsa are optimistic, overconfident, neither, or both.
