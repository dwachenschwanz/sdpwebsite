Panel discussion on Agile and Decision Quality / Decision Analysis

San Francisco Chapter Meeting: Speakers Carl Spetzler – Strategic Decisions Group (SDG), Himanshu Jain, Thought Agile Ltd., Audrey Del Vescovo – Chevron Corporation, Stephen Patch-Putt – Slalom Consulting
March 17, 2021

Panel discussion of the similarities and differences between Agile and Decision Quality / Decision Analysis and types of projects where they are applicable. The objective is to identify how the practices might benefit by considering the qualities of the other and improve decision making and outcomes.
