The AI Advantage: Embracing Artificial Intelligence in Decision Making

William Leaf‑Herrmann​​​​, Strategic Decisions Group, Partner
September 27, 2023 (SDP Invited Talk)

Abstract: In an era defined by rapid technological advancements, decision makers from various industries are increasingly turning to artificial intelligence (AI) to enhance their capabilities. But what is the true potential of AI in shaping alternatives for complex decisions? And how can AI be seamlessly integrated into human work to yield optimal results?

Join Strategic Decisions Group (SDG) in an exciting online webinar that explores the role of AI in strategy development while presenting a balanced perspective of its advantages and challenges in decision-making. Our event will feature experts from the life sciences and energy sectors providing valuable insights into the multifaceted world of AI in decision making.

 Key topics discussed are:

  • Strategic planning and long-term decision making supported by AI,
  • Mitigation of bias and ethical use of AI,
  • Trust, transparency, and organizational acceptance.
