Thinking about the Thinking 

Presented by Katherine Rosback â€“ KRE Inc.,

(SDP Webinar - Invited Talk)

Despite the well known value of having people come together to share ideas, explore options, and make decisions, complaints persist regarding lack of meeting focus and definitive outcomes. We tend to think that if we just get enough smart people in the room, good things will happen.  What is needed is a better understanding of the principles of meeting design and time spent thinking about how one wants the thinking to proceed in their meetings.  This webinar introduces a meeting design methodology that creates meeting focus, increases participant engagement, and ensures that the desired meeting outcomes are achieved.
