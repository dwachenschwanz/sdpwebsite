I learned about DA from That!

Presenter: Rodger Thompson, Pfizer

Presented at the 2017 DAAG Conference in New Orleans, Louisiana.  In Session 7: Coaching and Knowledge Transfer

Abstract: This talk will cover lessons learned from over 20 years of DA practice. Some of the tips that will be covered are:

  • Risky risk profiles
  • How to show a decision "bush” (don’t)
  • How to effectively share strategy tables with senior management
  • Optimal number of decision metrics to show
  • Making the point up front
  • Making sure to use the DA snaking process…
