I Love DA. I just wish we didn’t have to deal with people.

Presented by Stuart Harris – Decision Frameworks
(SDP Webinar - Invited Talk)

This is about how [lower] left brain decision analysts can deal with [upper] right brain clients. In this presentation, Harris will give examples of how communication that WE SEE AS REASONABLE as DA practitioners often has to change to successfully get the buy-in and understanding of those in need of a decision/solution.
