Creating a Personal Ethical Code

Ron Howard, Prof. Emeritus of the Department of Management Science and Engineering, School of Engineering, Stanford University
May 20, 2020 (Invited Talk)

Abstract: The webinar presents the distinctions necessary to create an effective personal ethical code. These include positive and negative ethical commitments, action-based and consequence-based ethics, the color of lies, business ethics, treating personal and professional secrets, the ethics of clinical trials, of discussions with a dying person, and a recent issue of ethical contamination. All concepts will be demonstrated by specific examples.
