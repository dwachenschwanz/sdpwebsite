Improving decision quality: one decision at a time

Karen Sepucha and Leigh Simmons, Health Decision Sciences Center, Mass. General Hospital
October 28, 2015 (SDP Webinar - Invited Talk)

In this presentation, Drs. Sepucha and Simmons will discuss their experiences trying to improve decision quality for patients facing significant medical decisions at Massachusetts General Hospital. They will summarize relevant literature around patient engagement and shared decision making and share successes and barriers to changing patient and clinician behaviors.
