On improving decision quality in medical sciences: or how I learned to stop worrying and love the medical professional

Eyas Raddad and Charles Benson, Eli Lilly
September 16, 2015 (SDP Webinar - Invited Talk)

Drs. Benson and Raddad have each spent 10+ years applying and championing quantitative approaches to medicine and drug development. They will describe their efforts in engaging coworkers / medical professionals in adopting these methods and share their personal experiences with tricks, traps, and expectations.
