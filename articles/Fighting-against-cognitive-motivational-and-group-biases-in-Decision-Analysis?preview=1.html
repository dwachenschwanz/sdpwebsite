Fighting against cognitive, motivational and group biases in Decision Analysis

Gilberto Montibeller, Prof. of Management Science, Loughborough University, UK
February 19, 2020 (Invited Talk)

Abstract: Following the success of our recent webinar on Biases in Decision Analysis (View the replay of "Biased Experts, Biased Decision Makers" ) we have organized a follow up, more intimate event, in which Profs Montibeller and Keisler will engage with the audience and discuss how we can fight against the biases that threaten the quality of our decision models and put at risk the quality of our recommendations. We will draw from cutting edge research in behavioural decision research and recent developments in the debiasing literature. The webinar will provide useful and practical advice to you and to your organization on how to fight against cognitive, motivational, and group biases.
