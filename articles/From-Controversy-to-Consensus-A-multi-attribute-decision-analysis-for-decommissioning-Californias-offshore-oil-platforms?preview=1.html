From Controversy to Consensus: A multi-attribute decision analysis for decommissioning California’s offshore oil platforms

Presenter: Max Henrion, Lumina Decision Systems

Abstract: Like many issues of energy and environment, there has been dispute about how to decommission California's 27 offshore oil platforms. Understand the decision analysis that provided stakeholders with insights to help form consensus for a rigs to reefs option.
Keywords: project framing, project evaluation, multi-objective, modeling, influence diagram, Monte Carlo simulation, stakeholders, environmental, swing weights, tornado chart, uncertainty analysis, public policy, societal decisions, sensitivity analysis
