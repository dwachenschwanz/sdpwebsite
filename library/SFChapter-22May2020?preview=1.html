San Francisco Chapter Meeting - May 2020

Decision Making Under Uncertainty in the Water Sector

Speaker: Thomas Chesnutt
May 22, 2020 (San Francisco Chapter Meeting)

Thomas discusses how an adequate accounting for uncertainty and risk requires not only new analytic approaches, but advances in both the way analysts communicate with policy-makers and in the way policy makers communicate with each other.
