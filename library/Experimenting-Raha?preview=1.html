Experimenting with Values: Finding Your Meta-Strategy 

Presented by: Somik Raha, SmartOrg Inc.
Jul 23 2014  (SDP Webinar - Invited Talk)

When we hear "values,” we interpret it in three different ways: cultural norms, metrics for tradeoffs (if you happen to be a decision analyst), or distinguishing between means and ends (if you’ve read "Value-Focused Thinking”). This talk will share a fourth way of hearing the term "values,” offering an interpretation of values as "meta-strategy” by understanding intrinsic purpose.
