SDP Webinar - Feb 2021

Virtual Decision-Modelling Workshops for Creating the Future

Presented by: Larry Phillips, Prof. Emeritus, London School of Economics, & Director, Facilitations Ltd.
With: Patrick Sharry, AGSM Fellow, Business School, UNSW, Australia.
Paul Gordon, CEO & Technical Director, Catalyze Asia Pacific.
Edward Poot, Director New Zealand, Catalyze Asia Pacific.
(SDP and DAS Joint Webinar - Invited Talk)

This Webinar describes how a group of experts can work collaboratively and virtually on contentious issues to create a decision-analytic model that will help individuals facing the issues in deciding what to do next.
