SDP Certification Level Details

Practitioner Certification Category Requirements

  • Practitioners must be elected by 2 Lead Practitioners (or Fellows) and a customer
  • Practitioners must submit the three references to the Society
  • The recommenders will attest to the fact that the applicant fits the "Practitioner” under Career Ladder

Lead Practitioner Certification Category Requirements

  • Lead Practitioners must be elected by 1 Fellow and 2 Lead Practitioners (or Fellows)
  • Lead Practitioners must submit the three references to the Society
  • The recommenders will attest to the fact that the applicant fits the "Lead Practitioner” description under Career Ladder

Fellow Certification Category Requirements

  • Fellows must submit a letter to the Society demonstrating their qualifications.
  • Fellows must be elected by three other fellows
  • The recommenders will attest to the fact that the applicant fits the "Fellow” description under Career Ladder
