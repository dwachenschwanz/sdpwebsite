University Programs - Normative

Based on Keeney, See, and von Winterfeldt's paper, here is a map of graduate academic programs in the prescriptive decision sciences.

Normative Decision Science Program Courses

Stanford University, Stanford, California

MS&E 52: Introduction to Decision Making, Prof. Burke Robinson
MS&E 152: Introducing Decision Analysis, Prof. Ross Shachter
MS&E 252: (DA1) Foundations of Decision Analysis, Prof. Ronald Howard
MS&E 352: (DA2) Professional Decision Analysis, Prof. Ronald Howard
MS&E 353: (DA3) Frontiers of Decision Analysis, Prof. Ronald Howard / Prof. James Matheson
MS&E 453: Decision Analysis Applications: Business Strategy and Public Policy, Prof. Burke Robinson
MS&E 454: Decision Analysis Projects: Helping Real Leaders Make Real Decisions, Prof. Burke Robinson
