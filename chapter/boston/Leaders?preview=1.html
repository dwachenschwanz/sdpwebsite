Meet Our Officers

 

WILLIAM LEAF-HERRMANN

President
 
William Leaf-Herrmann is a partner at LSC – LifeSciences Consultants, a global strategy consultancy supporting biopharma, MedTech, and diagnostics clients bring transformative healthcare products to the market. He is a Fellow in the Society of Decision Professionals and has been an active member since the society’s inception.

Vice President
 
Mikael Petrosyan is a decision-driven strategist in the life sciences industry. His expertise spans drug commercialization, portfolio management, and R&D strategy. He is a Practitioner in the Society of Decision Professionals.

MACIEJ KUKULA
