The SDP Denver Chapter was officially formed in December 2023. The Chapter’s vision is to bring together like-minded professionals to share knowledge, improve capacity and grow awareness of decision analysis, decision quality and structured decision making and its application. Chapter events are open to SDP members and non-members to attend and there is no charge to attend events.
