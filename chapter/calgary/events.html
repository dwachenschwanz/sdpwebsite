Events

Leaders

The Society of Decision Professionals Calgary (SDPC) Chapter was officially formed in November of 2017.  The chapter’s vision is to bring together like minded professionals to share knowledge, improve capacity, and grow awareness and implementation of decision analysis in the local industry and community.   The chapter is open to all interested parties who wish to participate.
