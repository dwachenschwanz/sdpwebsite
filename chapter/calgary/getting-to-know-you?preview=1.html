 Agenda

 

    Decision Analysis and Medical Decisions
   Larry Neal, Consultant & Past President of SDP

In 2012, Larry was diagnosed with prostate cancer.  He used decision analysis techniques to assist him in the selection of his treatment option.  In this talk he discusses that journey, his learnings, and the decision traps he encountered (Larry will be joining us via Skype)

   SDPC Directions for 2018
   Kent Burkholder, SDPC President

Kent will present a few slides on the 2018 chapter direction. We will include short group brainstorming sessions, to supplement our lists of both potential presentation topics, as well as other chapter initiatives.
