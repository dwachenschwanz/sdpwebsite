Meet Our Officers
 

 
JACK NEAL
President
EXXONMOBIL (RETIRED)
Details
Jack Neal is currently the VP / President-elect of the Houston Chapter of SDP.  He is a recently retired ExxonMobil executive after 28 years with the company.  During his career, Jack worked in upstream oil and gas exploration, development, production and research as a geoscientist, manager, and global senior advisor.

JAMES MAFFIONE
VP/President Elect
OLIN

James Maffione is the Capital and Strategic Evaluations Manager for Olin, the world’s leading producer of chlorine and membrane caustic soda, leading global supplier of epoxy materials, and top North American seller of chlorine bleach and hydrochloric acid. 

 



DIANA SWIFT
Secretary/Marketing Chair
Director of Brand, KCA

Diana Swift is the Director of Brand for K Carpenter Associates (KCA), a decision-focused consulting firm. She is a respected marketing professional with over 25 years of experience where she has held a variety of business development positions. These experiences enable her to present the business with realistic application of marketing practices, the management of KCA’s marketing strategies, and the activities done to drive a successful marketing plan.

President

Details
