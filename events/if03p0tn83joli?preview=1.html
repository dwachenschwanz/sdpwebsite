DQ CONFERENCE, NORWAY 2022 | September 7-8, 2022 (Workshops and Courses | 5-6 Sept, 2022)

REGISTER TODAY The Society of Decision Professionals (SDP) cordially invites you to attend the DQ CONFERENCE, NORWAY 2022 on September 7th – 8th, 2022.

Join leading decision professionals to learn how to “Make good decisions in an uncertain world”. The conference, In-kind sponsored by SDP, will be held in-person at Soria Moria Hotel & Conference Center in Oslo. Registration is now open at https://dqnorway.com/

Explore why it is hard to make decisions under uncertainty and how to succeed. Gain meaningful insight into how you can improve your own and your organization’s decision-making practices and how to build the six elements of DQ. Agenda includes talks from experienced Decision Professionals on decision framing, data analytics and decision making, and information quality.

WHO SHOULD ATTEND
Leaders and new and existing practitioners that want to impact decision-making in an ever-changing world.

PRE-CONFERENCE WORKSHOPS
A unique lineup of workshops will be offered two days before the DQ Conference, Norway 2022, September 5th – 6th 2022. Visit the website to find complete schedules of the conference and workshops.

**software roadshow included in the conference where you can learn about market-leading tools within the field of Decision Analysis.**
