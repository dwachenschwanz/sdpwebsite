About

Australasia chapter of the Society of Decision Professionals was formed in 2021. The chapter's vision is to bring together like minded professionals to share knowledge, improve capacity, and grow awareness and implementation of decision analysis in the local industry and community. The chapter is open to all interested parties who wish to participate.
