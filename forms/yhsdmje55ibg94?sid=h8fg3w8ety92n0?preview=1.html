Decision Intelligence Confronts A.I.

 

Decision Making is a complex human process. AI seems to be taking over the world, but does it make a real contribution? On November 10th, we came together to discuss ways to move the ball on how AI, Data Science and Machine Learning can really contribute to making Decisions more Intelligent. 

"Decision Intelligence" brings together the data-driven analytics of Data Science within the larger context of decision modeling...to bring maturity to the practice of Data Science and extend the data driven reach of Decision Analysis.

HERE'S WHAT WE LEARNED ABOUT IN OUR 1 DAY ONLINE EVENT: 

•About Decision Science
•About AI / ML / Data Science
•Cases that show progress
•Methods in the middle
