Decision Education (Interest Group)


The Decision Education Interest Group is a collective of decision professionals dedicated to advancing the understanding and practice of personal decision-making across all age groups, from K-12 to adults. We aim to integrate decision education into both formal and informal settings, transforming the way decisions are approached and enhancing the quality of choices made in all aspects of life. Our mission is to change the conversation around decisions, equipping individuals with the tools and knowledge to navigate life's choices with confidence and clarity.
